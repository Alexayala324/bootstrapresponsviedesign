How it all started

Kanye was born in Chicago, Illinois and from a young age, showed promise in music. During his time in 7th grade, he started selling musical compositions to other artists and even wrote a rap song called, "Green Eggs and Ham." At the age of 20, he dropped out of college and soon created his first album.

Kanye's albums during the 2000s

At the beginning of Kanye's career, his albums were making headlines and his remarks to the general public were as well. His mother had also passed away which left a long lasting effect on him. His albums were using themes of love, loneliness, and heartache.

Yeezy's 2010s transition

During this era, we start to witness the change in his style and demeanor. Kanye begins to comfront those who had gone  against him with the only way he knows how, music.He gets married and starts having kids while changing the themes behind his music with focusing more on celebrity and excess.     

 

Kanye 2020

This year was a tumultous one for Kanye and his mental health, but through it came a new sound of gospel music and a new awakening. While 2020 was a tumultous year for everyone around the world, Kanye proceeded with some of his unfortunate antics, like running for president.

Kanye's services and future projects

His music has transcended to the stage and through his other works.He started performing sunday services with his choir and started speaking about things that really matter to him.
